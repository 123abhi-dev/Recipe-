<h1>TEHRI</h1>
<p>This is mixture of rice with other vegetables cooked thoroughly with pinch of salt and masalas</p>
<h2> Ingredients </h2>
<ul>
<li>Rice</li>
<li>Water</li>
<li>potato</li>
<li>Onion</li>
<li>Garlic</li>
<li>Masala</li>
<li>Tomato</li>
</ul>
<h2>Instructions</h2>
<ol>
<li>Wash the rice </li>
<li>Chop the vegetables</li>
<li>Heat oil or ghee</li>
<li>Add Whole Spices</li>
<li>Cook the Onions</li>
<li>Add Tomatoes and Masalas</li>
<li>Add Vegetables</li>
<li>Mix in the Rice</li>
<li>Add Water and Cook</li>
<li>Serve Hot </li>
</ol>
